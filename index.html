Introduction

My name is Rishabh Singh Teja.
I am currently a Senior Research Fellow (SRF) at the Indian Institute of Astrophysics (IIA) situated at Bangalore , Karnataka, India.
I have completed my two and half years of PhD here.

I work on the observational and theoretical aspects of Core-Collapse Supernovae (CCSNe), which are the spectacular deaths of stars with initial masses greater than 8 Suns combined.
Till now I have worked on the data from the following telescopes:

  • Himalayan Chandra Telescope (2-m)
  • Devasthal Optical Telescope (3.6-m)
  • UVOT onboard Swift Observatory
  • Growth India Telescope
