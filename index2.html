Introduction

My name is Rishabh Singh Teja. I am currently a Senior Research Fellow (JRF) at the Indian Institute of Astrophysics (IIA) situated at Bangalore , Karnataka, India. I have completed my two years of PhD here.

I work on the observational studies of core-collapse Supernovae. Till now I have worked on the data from the following telescopes:

  • Chandra Telescope (2-m)
  • Devasthal Optical Telescope (3.6-m)
  • UVOT onboard Swift Observatory
  • Growth India Telescope
